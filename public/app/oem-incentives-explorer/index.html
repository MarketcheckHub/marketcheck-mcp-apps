What This App Does

A dark-themed dashboard for browsing and comparing OEM incentive programs across major brands. Pick a make (and optionally a model and ZIP), then see all active manufacturer offers — cash back, low-APR financing, lease specials, loyalty cash, and conquest bonuses — as color-coded cards with amounts, eligible models, expiration countdowns, and fine print. Add up to two competing brands for a side-by-side comparison table that highlights the best deal per incentive type. A built-in savings calculator lets shoppers stack eligible cash incentives against an MSRP and instantly see the effective price plus an estimated monthly payment at the best APR on offer.

Who Can Use This

Car Shoppers
Check what manufacturer money is on the hood before walking into a dealership — stack loyalty, cash back, and conquest cash against MSRP to see the real out-the-door price.
Cross-Shoppers
Add up to two competing brands (e.g., Toyota vs Honda vs Hyundai) and get an at-a-glance comparison of who offers the best APR, lease, or cash-back deal right now.
Dealer Sales Staff
Keep a tab open while working with customers to quickly recall current OEM programs, stackability rules, and expiration dates without digging through bulletin PDFs.
Auto Journalists & Content Creators
Pull current incentive data for end-of-month deal roundups, best-lease-deal articles, and holiday sales-event coverage.
Lenders & Fleet Buyers
Factor OEM cash and subvented APR into effective loan amounts and fleet acquisition cost comparisons.

Pass parameters directly in the URL to pre-fill the form and auto-generate the report:

2. As an MCP App (AI Assistants)

    "marketcheck-apps": {

  }
}

Then ask the AI: "Use the oem-incentives-explorer tool to cash back, apr, and lease deals by zip"
